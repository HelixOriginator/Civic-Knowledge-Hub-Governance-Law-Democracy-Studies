# Civic Knowledge Hub: Governance, Law & Democracy Studies

## Overview

This repository brings together open-access research and educational resources focused on governance, constitutional law, democracy, and public policy. It is designed to support civic literacy, critical thinking, and accessible understanding of complex political and legal concepts.

## Contents

* Research articles on constitutional, historical, and socio-political narratives
* Plain-language civic education resources
* Analytical frameworks for evaluating governance and public discourse
* Educational tools for students, researchers, and general readers

## Purpose

The goal of this repository is to make high-quality civic knowledge accessible in clear and practical language. It bridges academic research and public understanding, enabling informed participation in democratic processes.
